10 Phase 3 Deep Dive

From bookkeeper to bank

Phase 1 = SME relationship (free software). Phase 2 = transaction data (payments). Phase 3 = monetization (wallet + lending).

Real Turnover-Based Lending

Underwrite from actual payment activity — not credit scores, BEE certificates, or bank statements. Real-time revenue visibility. Instant approval, auto-repayment from future transactions.

Revenue: Net interest margin (15–25% APR)

Stablecoin Savings & USD Yield

SMEs save in USDC/USDT to hedge ZAR volatility. Idle business cash earns 5–8% USD yield vs 0.5% locally. Simple "savings account" interface.

Revenue: Management fee (20% of yield) + FX spread

Stablecoin Payments

USDC/USDT rails for cross-border payments at near-zero fees. SA businesses importing from China, Nigeria, Kenya. Transparent FX rates vs 5% bank spreads. Settlement in minutes, not days.

Revenue: FX spread (0.5–1%) + float yield

Credit & Tax Products

Invoice factoring (advance cash on outstanding invoices). Automated VAT calculation and SARS-ready returns. Business insurance underwritten by real revenue.

Revenue: Factoring fees + insurance commission + tax prep SaaS
