10 — Phase 3 Deep Dive

From bookkeeper to bank

Phase 1 gives us the SME relationship (free software). Phase 2 gives us transaction data (payments). Phase 3 monetises both with a PopPay wallet for businesses and consumers — we know every invoice, every payment, every cash flow cycle. That's a credit file no bank has.

Real Turnover-Based Lending

Underwrite loans from actual payment activity — not credit scores, not BEE certificates, not 3 months of bank statements. We see real-time revenue. Instant approval, repaid automatically from future transactions. This is the lending product SA SMEs have never had.

Revenue: Net interest margin (15–25% APR)

Stablecoin Savings & USD Yield

SMEs save in USDC/USDT to hedge ZAR volatility. Idle business cash earns 5–8% USD yield vs 0.5% at a local bank — abstracted behind a simple "savings account" interface. No crypto knowledge needed.

Revenue: Management fee (20% of yield) + FX spread

Stablecoin Payments

USDC/USDT rails for cross-border payments at near-zero fees. SA businesses importing from China, Nigeria, Kenya — pay suppliers in stablecoins instead of SWIFT. Transparent FX rates, not the 5% spread banks charge. Settlement in minutes, not days.

Revenue: FX spread (0.5–1%) + float yield

Credit & Tax Products

Invoice factoring (advance cash against outstanding invoices). Automated VAT calculation and SARS-ready returns. Business insurance underwritten by real revenue. Each product is a natural extension of data we already have.
